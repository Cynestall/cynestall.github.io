Info


How can you help me?

We help our customers build the best performance PC for their money.

Why can't I just look for parts on my own?

We encourage you to do so, but if you are uncertain then we are always here to help.
Professionals hired by us pick the components and base them on the best performance for a certain budget.

How can I use it?

You can search for a PC depending on your budget on our website in the Recommendations tab or download our app from the app store.

How many price ranges are covered?

While we are not able to cover all budgets, we have stereotypical ones picked out.
If you wish to get a professional to pick parts for your budget, you can send an email to pcrequest@pcrec.com.
