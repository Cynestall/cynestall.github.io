What is Feeder?

Feeder will help you decide where and what you want to eat based on your location, your price range and what you are in the mood for for example what type of food you like.

For who?

The app can be used by anyone who wants to go out and eat but does not know what to eat or where and what to order in a specific restaurant. This app is meant mostly for teens and adults.
